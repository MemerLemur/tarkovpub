FAQ

Is this server beginner friendly?

Yes. If you're willing to learn and keep it respectful, you'll fit right in.

What does "no-ego" actually mean here?

No flaming teammates, no gatekeeping, no weird superiority. Critique is fine — disrespect isn't.

How do I get into squads fast?

Join → introduce yourself → use LFG → react to a few clips → post one of your own. People engage with active members.

How does the Friday clip contest work?

Post clips throughout the week. Clips need reactions to count (no reacting to your own clip). Winner is based on community reactions/opinions to keep it quality-driven.
